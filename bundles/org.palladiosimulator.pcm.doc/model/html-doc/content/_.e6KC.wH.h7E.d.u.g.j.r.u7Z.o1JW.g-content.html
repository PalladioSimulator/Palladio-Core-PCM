Zuordnung <Unnamed Association>

An EntryLevelSystemCall models the call to a service provided by a system. Therefore, an
EntryLevelSystemCall references a Signature specifying the called service. This is realised by this association.

Each EntryLevelSystemCall references exacly one Signature of an Interface used in the System. The other direction is not navigable, as the Inertface and Signature have no notion about referencing UsageScenarios and UsageModels.
