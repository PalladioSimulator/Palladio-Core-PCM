Zuordnung <Unnamed Association>

The sequence of AbstractUserActions (i.e. actions within a Scenariobehaviour) is realised with this association. Each AbstractUserAction can have a maximum of one successor and a maximum of one predecessor. Most user actions have both, only Start actions have just a successor and Stop actions have just a predecessor.

An actions knows both its successor and predeccessor, which makes the sequence navigable in both directions. Branches and Loops are realised in nested ScenarioBehaviours of the respective actions. Thus, this association only realises sequences, no other trees or graphs.
